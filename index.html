Your Schedule

Practice in the morning, at lunch, or in the evening — no studio timetable required.

Minimal Setup

All you need is a mat and 15–20 minutes of space near a wall or on the floor.

Your Own Pace

Pause, rest, or repeat a pose as many times as your body needs.

Where to Start

Home yoga can support flexibility, balance, and a calming routine — but you do not need to learn every pose at once. Pick one short sequence and repeat it when it fits your schedule. Individual experiences vary.

First step: 5 minutes of calm breathing seated on your mat.

Basic Poses

A 20-Minute Practice Structure

5 min — warm-up. Gentle neck, shoulder, and spine movements while standing.

10 min — main poses. 3–4 positions at your level (details on the Practice page).

5 min — cool-down. Resting relaxation with attention on your breath.

This format fits easily into a weekday and you can gradually extend it when you are ready.

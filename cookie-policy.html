Your Choices (U.S. Consent)

On your first visit, our cookie banner lets you:

  • Accept All — enable essential, analytics, and marketing cookies
  • Reject — disable non-essential cookies (only strictly necessary cookies remain)
  • Cookie Settings — choose Analytics and Marketing categories individually

Strictly necessary cookies cannot be disabled because the site will not function correctly without them. You may withdraw consent later by clearing browser storage for our site or contacting us. Where required by law, we honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) and similar opt-out preference signals as a request to opt out of sale/sharing for targeted advertising.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember preferences, understand usage, and improve performance.

Cookie Categories

Category Purpose Can you opt out?
Strictly Necessary Security, load balancing, form operation, consent storage No (required)
Analytics Understand traffic and improve content (aggregated where possible) Yes — Reject or Cookie Settings
Marketing / Affiliate Measure affiliate referrals and relevant promotions Yes — Reject or Cookie Settings

Sale, Sharing, and Targeted Advertising

Under some U.S. state privacy laws, certain analytics or advertising cookies may be considered “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not sell your personal information for money. If you opt out via our banner, GPC signal, or a privacy request, we disable optional cookies used for those purposes where technically feasible.

Retention of Cookie Data

Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies and consent records are typically stored for up to 12 months unless a shorter period applies in your browser settings.

Browser Controls

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may prevent forms or consent tools from working. Learn more at your browser’s help pages or allaboutcookies.org.

Third-Party Technologies

Embedded Google Maps, affiliate retailer links, and external apps may set their own cookies when you interact with them. We do not control third-party technologies. Review Google’s and each retailer’s policies before use.

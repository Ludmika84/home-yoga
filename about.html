About Us

Yoga at Home is an educational resource based in Tulsa, Oklahoma. We create clear materials for anyone who wants to practice at home without complex terminology, expensive memberships, or pressure to keep up with a class.

Clear Materials

Step-by-step pose descriptions in plain language — no unnecessary jargon, with a focus on safety and how it feels in your body.

Safe Progress

Our focus is gradual development at a comfortable pace. Slow and steady practice supports long-term habits.

Practice at Home

A mat and 15–20 minutes are enough. Practice whenever it works — morning, midday, or before bed.

Four Principles

Consistency Over Intensity

Fifteen minutes daily beats two hours once a month. The body adapts through repetition.

Breath as Your Guide

If your breath becomes shallow or strained — reduce the range of motion or pause in Child's Pose.

Gradual Progression

Start with basic poses, then add energizing flows and deeper relaxation.

Respect for Your Body

Discomfort during movement is a signal to pause or modify a pose. We encourage mindful practice rather than forcing range of motion.

Who We Write For

Our materials are designed for people with little or no yoga experience. If you already attend a studio, our programs can complement your practice at home — but they are not a substitute for in-person guidance on advanced poses.

Our materials are educational only. If you have injuries, are pregnant, or manage a health condition, speak with a qualified healthcare provider before starting.
